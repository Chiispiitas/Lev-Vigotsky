Place your music files here with these exact names:

- title.mp3   → looped; starts as soon as the website loads when the browser allows autoplay, continues through title/menu and stops when the first round starts. It does not play on the podium screen.
- round.mp3   → looped; plays during each question round and stops when answer results are shown.
- podium.mp3  → non-looped; plays once when the podium screen appears. It is the only music/sound track used on the podium screen.

Browser note: some browsers block unmuted autoplay until the first click/key press. The game still requests title.mp3 immediately on load and retries after the first interaction.
